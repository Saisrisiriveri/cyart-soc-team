**Incident Response Process**

**Overview**

Incident response is a structured approach to handle security incidents.

**Phases of Incident Response**

Preparation

Identification

Containment  
Eradication

Recovery  
Lessons Learned

1.  **Preparation  
    **Prepare tools, policies, and training.
2.  **Identification  
    **Detect and confirm the incident.
3.  **Containment  
    **Limit damage by isolating systems.
4.  **Eradication  
    **Remove threats from the system.
5.  **Recovery  
    **Restore systems to normal operation.
6.  **Lessons Learned  
    **Analyze incident and improve security.

**Example:**

**Phishing Attack**

- Identify suspicious email
- Block sender
- Remove email from inbox
- Educate user

**Conclusion**

Following these steps ensures effective incident handling.
